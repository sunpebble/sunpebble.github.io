Privacy Policy

Effective July 2, 2026

The short version

Dayroll collects nothing. We can't read your journal, and we don't want to.

Your data

Everything you write in Dayroll is stored locally on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, entries are synced through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit — encrypted in transit and at rest, accessible only to you. Your data never touches our servers, because we don't have any.

What we don't do

No accounts. No analytics. No ads. No third-party SDKs. No tracking of any kind.

Purchases

The one-time unlock is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
