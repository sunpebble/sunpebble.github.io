Restore your purchase

Reinstalled or got a new phone? Open Sleeptab → Settings → Restore Purchase. The unlock is tied to your Apple ID, so you'll never pay twice.

No sleep data showing?

Sleeptab needs permission to read sleep from Apple Health. Check Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Sleeptab, and make sure your watch or sleep tracker is writing sleep to Health.

Where is my data?

On your device, always. See the Privacy Policy.
